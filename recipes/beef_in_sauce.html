Beef in sauce

Description

Beef in sauce is one of the most traditional meat recipes in latin america. This recipe varies depending on where you are located in the world, but this is a very basic and simple implementation of this dish.

    Ingredients
  • Onion
  • Beef
  • Green Pepper
  • Carrot
  • Broccoli
  • Garlic
  • Natural Tomato
  • Tomato Paste
  • Red Wine
  • Beef Broth
  • Flour
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil
    Steps
  1. Season the meat with salt and pepper and coat in flour
  2. Remove excess flour and brown in a wide saucepan with oil at the base.
  3. When meat is golden brown, remove and set aside.
  4. Dice the vegetables and cook them over low heat for about 20 minutes.
  5. Add the natural tomato and the tomato pase and cook for another 10 minutes.
  6. Return meat to the pot and add the wine. Turn up the heat and let the alcohol evaporate.
  7. Cover with beef broth and let it cook for 1 hour over low heat
  8. Add broccoli and cook for another 20 minutes.
  9. Enjoy!
