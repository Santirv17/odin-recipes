Pad Thai

Description

Pad Thai is a stir-fry dish made with rice noodles, protein, and other ingredients sauteed together in a wok and tossed in a Pad Thai Sauce. It's a common street food in Thailand and one of the most popular menu items at Thai restaurants around the country.

    Ingredients
  • Rice Noodles
  • Shrimp, chicken, or tofu
  • Peanuts
  • Eggs
  • Bean Sprouts
  • Fish Sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Sugar
  • Tamarind Paste
  • Soy Sauce
  • Garlic
  • Bell Pepper
  • Green Onions
  • Cilantro
  • Lime
    Steps
  1. Cook Noodles by following the package instructions until tender. Rinse under cold water
  2. Make Sauce by stirring together fish sauce, soy sauce, brown sugar, and tamarind paste. Set aside.
  3. Heat 1.5 tablespoons of oil in a laurge saucepan over medium-high heat. Add your protein, garlic, and bell pepper. Cook until meat is done.
  4. Push everything to the side of the pan, add a little more oil, and scramble eggs by breaking them with a spatula as they cook.
  5. Add noodles, sauce, bean sprouts, and peanuts to the pan. Toss everything to combine.
  6. Serve with green onions, extra peanuts, cilantro, and lime wedges.
  7. Enjoy!
