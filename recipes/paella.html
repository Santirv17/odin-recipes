Paella

Description

Paella is a classic Spanish rice dish made with rice, saffron, vegetables, chicken, and seafood cooked and served all in one pan. It's recognized as the national food of spain.

    Ingredients
  • Onion
  • Bell Pepper
  • Garlic
  • Tomatoes
  • Parsley
  • Peas
  • Bay Leaf
  • Paprika
  • Saffron
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Jumbo Shrimp
  • Mussels
  • Calamari
  • Chicken Thighs
  • Spanish Olive Oil
  • White WIne
  • Spanish Rice
  • Chicken Broth
    Steps
  1. Add olive oil to a skillet over medium heat. Add onion, bell peppers, and garlic and saute until onion is translucent. Add chopped tomato, bay leaf, paprika, saffron, salt, and pepper. Stir and cook for 5 minutes.
  2. Add white wine and cook for 10 minutes.
  3. Add chicken, rice, and chopped parsley and cook for 1 minute.
  4. Pout the broth slowly around the pan and jiggle the pan to get the rice into an even layer. Bring mixture to a boil. Reduce heat to medium low. Give the pan gentle shakes while cooking.
  5. Cook paella uncovered for 15-18 minutes, then nestle the shrimp, mussels, and calamari into the mixture, spinkle peas on top, and continue to cook for 5 minutes. Watch for most of the liquid to be absorbed and rice on top nearly tender.
  6. Remove pan from heat and cover with a lid or tinfoil. Let rest for 10 minutes.
  7. Enjoy!
